Code of Conduct

Definitions:

  1. Owner: Dovuq

Team scoring rules:

  1. If there is any conflict among the rules, the one with the smallest number shall prevail.
  2. The initial score is 256 points.
  3. If the suggestion is adopted by the administrator, 1~32 points will be added according to the importance.
  4. For those who make excessive suggestions and brush points, 64 points will be deducted once they are found out. If they are found out for the second time, the administrator qualification will be cancelled.
  5. There is no upper limit to the score.
  6. If the score is below 0 for 30 consecutive days, you will be kicked out of the team.
  7. You can enter the team 30 days after you're kicked out, but you can never enter after you're kicked out again.
  8. The score is not transferable.
  9. If you have any questions about the score change, you can send it to the owner within 30 days.
  10. For other situations, the owner will add -16~16 points or kick out of the team. Rule 7 does not apply if you are kicked out of the team.
  11. Whoever impersonates the owner and publishes news outside will be punished according to the seriousness of the plot.
  12. All penalties will be shown in the note column of the scoreboard.
  13. In case of doubt, the owner shall have the right of interpretation.
  14. Those who have not scored for a long time in a row will show Brown names and will not participate in the ranking.
  15. Look forward to other rules.

Administrator rules:

  1. The owner is a permanent administrator.
  2. Administrators need real name authentication.
  3. The administrator can change the article at will.
  4. After changing the article, you should send a message to the owner, otherwise you may not get bonus points.
  5. You cannot change code of conduct, points or the personal home page of any user other than yourself. Once found, 100 points will be deducted and the administrator qualification will be cancelled.
  6. Those who have not scored for one year in a row will be disqualified as administrator.
  7. Administrator qualification can be abandoned, but if you want to have administrator qualification again after giving up, you must apply to the owner.
  8. If an administrator kicks the owner out of the team, he will never be allowed into all the owner's teams.
  9. Look forward to other rules.
