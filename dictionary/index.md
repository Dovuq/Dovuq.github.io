        <h1>This is Dovuq's Dictionary.</h1>
        <strong>Tips:</strong>
        <p>Verbs has different tences. There are Normeno, Fixano, Pastino, Omegano, and Futuro.
            The verb will write in Normeno. And other tences will write after it.
            For example: <code>sieque (siequan, sieque, sieque, siequ)</code>
            Some verbs doesn't have some tences. They will be written <code>/</code></p>
        <p>Noun's plural: usually -(e)s</p>
        <p>Adjective's comparative: usually -ji</p>
        <p>Adjective's superlative: usually -jest</p>
